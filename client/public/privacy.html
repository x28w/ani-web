Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

ani-web helps users discover anime, watch episodes, and keep track of viewing progress. Guests may use the application without connecting a Google account.

Information Stored

A guest session cookie may be used so watch activity remains separate from other visitors. Watch progress and watchlist activity may be stored by the ani-web installation to provide those features when a user returns.

Profile display names and profile picture customizations are stored locally in the user's browser on that device.

Optional Telemetry

If enabled in Settings, ani-web may store an anonymous installation ID, app version, last-seen time, and general browser type. Watch history is not included in telemetry.

Google Drive Synchronization

If Google Drive synchronization is connected, ani-web accesses the connected Google profile and Drive storage only to identify the account and synchronize ani-web application data. Google data is not sold or used for advertising.

Users can revoke Google access from their Google Account permissions settings.
